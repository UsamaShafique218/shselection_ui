About

SHSelection From Legacy Retail to Smart Commerce 50 years+ of brands, markets, concepts and transformation SHSelection was founded in 1972 by Wolf Jochen Schulte-Hillen.

What began as a sales-driven business evolved over the decades into an internationally oriented consulting and network platform focused on brand building, retail expansion, usage concepts and location development. Over more than five decades, my work has combined international retail expansion, brand and business development, and the creation of forward-looking usage concepts for owners, investors and developers.

Selected expansion-related assignments included retail and market development activities connected with Benetton Sportsystem, BoConcept, EB Games, Habitat, MUJI, Uniqlo, Pepe Jeans, S.Oliver France, Sephora, Skechers, The Phone House and ZARA. A further core field of activity was the strategic development and positioning of retail and mixed-use destinations in Germany and internationally. In Germany, this included major projects in Berlin, Frankfurt, Hamburg, Leipzig, Münster, Munich and Emsdetten. Internationally, mandates and project involvement extended to Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Cairo, New York, Paris, Strasbourg and Tokyo.

My brand and business development work covered a broad international spectrum of fashion, sportswear, footwear, cosmetics, home, lifestyle and department store businesses. Selected names include Benetton Sportsystem, Body Glove, Converse, El Corte Inglés, Ellesse, Kappa, Karstadt, MUJI, Nordica, Otto Group, Pepe Jeans, Sephora, Skechers, UNIQLO and ZARA / Inditex, among many others.

This work across categories and markets provided deep experience in brand positioning, market entry, retail adaptation, concept development and international growth strategy.

In more recent years, I have also focused on autonomous stores and AI-driven retail concepts. Since the opening of Amazon Go in Seattle in 2016, and the rapid expansion of frictionless retail formats in China and Japan, I have closely followed, analysed and presented on these developments. I also advised selected German initiatives in this field and continue to engage with autonomous stores, AI-supported retail solutions and intelligent fridge concepts as part of my ongoing advisory work.
