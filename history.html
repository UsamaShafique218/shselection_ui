SHSelection – International Advisory & Brand Expertise

Representative International Mandates and Advisory Activities

More than 50 years successfully focusing on innovative international product and retail branding.

SHSelection was founded in 1972 by Wolf Jochen Schulte-Hillen and developed from a distribution company into an internationally active consulting platform for brand building, retail expansion, usage concepts and location development.

Wolf Jochen Schulte-Hillen, lawyer and businessman, is regarded as one of Germany’s most experienced retail and brand experts, with more than five decades of entrepreneurial and advisory practice.

During the 1970s, 1980s and 1990s, SHSelection supported the development of more than 60 international lifestyle, sports and retail brands in sales, marketing and distribution.

Since 1996, the company has focused on international consulting for retail, lifestyle, entertainment and real estate businesses, as well as on developing future-oriented usage and expansion concepts.

Athletic Excellence & Personal Achievements

In addition to his entrepreneurial work, Wolf Jochen Schulte-Hillen was a highly successful competitive athlete. He ranked among Germany’s leading 1,500-metre runners and qualified for the 1968 Olympic Games in Mexico.

Together with his relay teammates Norpoth and Kemper, he still holds the oldest German relay record in the 3 x 1000 metre relay — a remarkable achievement in German athletics history.
